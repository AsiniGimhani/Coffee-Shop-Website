Our History

Our journey began with a simple dream — to share the love of great coffee with our community. What started as a small family café in Sri Lanka has grown into a cozy spot where friends gather and new memories are made every day. From our early days of hand-brewing cups for our first customers to serving a variety of blends and treats today, we’ve always believed that good coffee brings people together.
